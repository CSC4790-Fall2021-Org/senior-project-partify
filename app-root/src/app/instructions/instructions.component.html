1. Find the playlist you would like to Partify
  2. Select "Edit Further" if you would like to add or remove songs from your playlist
  3. Select how you want to sort your playlist:
    • Danceability - If you want your playlist to group together songs you can dance to
    • BPM - If you want your playlist to group songs based on their pace
    • Energy - If you want your playlist to group together loud and fast songs
    • Camelot Key - If you want your playlist to transition from one key to its neighbor
